The Evolution of Computers

-2500

abacus

the first known calculator; laid the foundations for positional notation.

1642

Pascaline

mechanical calculator with a controlled carry mechanism.

1672

Stepped Reckoner

could multiply numbers of up to 12 digits to give a 16 digit result.

1804

jacquard loom

an automatic loom controlled by punched cards.

1822

difference engine

an automatic mechanical calculator designed to tabulate polynomial functions.

1833

Analytical Engine

mechanical general-purpose computer that could be described in modern terms as Turing-complete.
